Session Content Preview: A Deeper Dive

Our training will provide a comprehensive look at the multifaceted dangers of Artificial Intelligence. We will move beyond the headlines to explore the technical underpinnings and real-world implications of AI risks.

  • Module 1: The Illusion of Reality: Delve into the world of generative AI, focusing on deepfakes in audio, video, and text. Learn how to spot synthetic media and understand its potential for manipulation and fraud.
  • Module 2: Your Data, Their Power: Examine how AI systems collect, process, and potentially misuse personal data. We'll discuss privacy breaches, surveillance risks, and strategies for digital self-defense.
  • Module 3: The Unseen Hand of Bias: Uncover how biases in training data can lead to discriminatory outcomes in AI applications, from hiring algorithms to facial recognition. Learn to critically evaluate AI's fairness.
  • Module 4: Societal Shifts & Ethical Frontiers: A broader discussion on AI's impact on employment, legal frameworks, and human autonomy. We'll explore the ethical dilemmas and the importance of responsible AI development.
  • Interactive Q&A and Practical Tips: Conclude with an open forum for questions and a session dedicated to actionable steps you can take to protect yourself and advocate for safer AI.

This session is designed to be engaging and informative, empowering you with the knowledge to navigate the AI landscape securely.

What You'll Learn: Critical Insights

  • Deepfakes & Misinformation: How AI can create convincing fake content and spread false narratives.
  • Privacy & Data Security: The risks of AI systems collecting and using your personal data.
  • Algorithmic Bias: Understanding how AI can perpetuate and amplify societal biases.
  • Job Displacement & Ethical Concerns: The broader societal impacts and moral dilemmas of advanced AI.
  • Practical Safeguards: Actionable steps you can take to protect yourself and your family.
